The main functions of a certain tourism information management system include:
1. User management: including the management of user names, passwords, avatars, nicknames and other information. Users can write guides.
2. Guide management: including the management of guide titles, categories, tags, content and other information.
3. Hotel management: including the management of hotel names, introductions, pictures, locations and other information.
4. Hotel management: including the management of hotel names, introductions, pictures, locations and other information.
5. Attraction management: including the management of attraction names, introductions, pictures, addresses, opening hours, tips, days and other information.
6. Route management: including the management of route names, content, tags, introductions and other information.
