The land project management system shall meet the following business requirements:

The land management department manages multiple land parcels. Each land parcel has a Land ID, Land Location, and Total Land Area. 

The department manages multiple projects. Each project has a Project ID, Project Name, Project Location, Purpose, and Person in Charge.

Each land usage record shall capture the relationship between the land and project, including Used Land Area, Usage Duration, Current Status, Record Time, and Recording Personnel. 

By recording the usage of land by projects, the system shall ensure reasonable allocation and effective management of land resources. 

A project can use multiple land parcels, and a land parcel can be used by multiple projects successively over time.
