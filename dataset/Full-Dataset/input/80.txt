The operational management of a certain automotive repair company is as follows: The company needs to manage customer information, which includes the customer's name and phone number. Customers can contact the repair service by phone. The company also manages the information of repair workers, including their names and addresses. During the repair process, parts are used, and the information about these parts includes serial number, description, price, and supplier, as well as the management of the inventory quantity of the parts. Each repair task has detailed information, including the involved vehicle model, task status, customer information, responsible repair worker, task start date, and completion date. The task status can be "pending," "in progress," or "completed." Each repair task may require multiple parts, so it is necessary to record the parts needed for each task and their quantities.
