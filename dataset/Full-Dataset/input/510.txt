System: Parking lot management system

Description of required functions:
1. Parking space management:
The system queries available parking spaces, and when a vehicle enters, it marks the parking space as occupied.
When a vehicle exits from the exit, it marks the parking space as empty.

2. Parking space owner management:
The system needs to record the information of certain car owners who have purchased parking spaces, including their license plate number, name, and contact information.
