An e-commerce platform sells e-cigarette products:
Users can register by providing their name, phone number, address, and payment method.
The platform sells various e-cigarette products, each with a unique ID, including the manufacturer, flavor, number of puffs, unit price, and stock quantity.
Users can add products to their shopping cart, recording the quantity of selected products.
Users can select multiple products in their shopping cart for checkout, generate an order, and document the order creation time and order status.
