The business requirements for the company's equipment management system are as follows:
The company owns multiple pieces of equipment, each equipped with an equipment ID, name, model, and usage status.
The company also employs several maintenance personnel, each with a unique maintenance personnel ID, name, telephone, and email.
Whenever equipment requires maintenance, a maintenance record is generated, which includes the equipment ID, maintenance time, maintenance results, and the personnel responsible for the maintenance.
The system needs to manage equipment information, maintenance personnel information, and equipment maintenance records to ensure the tracking of each piece of equipment's maintenance history and responsible personnel.
