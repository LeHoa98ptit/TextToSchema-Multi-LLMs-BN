The daily-use products sales company sells home appliances, furniture, and other daily-use products. The company has multiple sales teams, each consisting of several sales personnel. Each sales task is identified by a task ID and includes customer information (customer name, customer telephone), product information (product name, model, price), sales date, sales quantity, and sales cost. After completing a sale, the customer evaluation must be recorded. The performance evaluation of sales personnel is based on the number of sales tasks completed and customer satisfaction.
