Database name: School

This database is a school management system used to record student, teacher and subject information, as well as manage and record students' test scores and subject scores.

Main functions

1. Student management:
Record students' personal information, including name, age, address and phone number.
Manage the relationship between students and subjects, and record students' scores in various subjects.
Record the exams students take and their scores.

2. Subject management:
Record the name and number of class hours of each subject.
Manage the relationship between subjects and students and teachers.

3. Exam management:
Record the date of the exam and the corresponding subject.
Manage students' exam scores.

4. Teacher management:
Record teachers' personal information, including name, address and phone number.
Manage the subjects taught by teachers.
Record the guidance relationship between teachers and students.

5. Grade management:
Record students' subject scores and test scores.
Provide grade query and statistical functions, such as querying all grades of a student, the average grade of all students in a subject, etc.

6. Relationship management:
Manage the relationship between students and subjects, students and teachers, and teachers and subjects.
For example, you can query which teachers teach which students in which subjects.
