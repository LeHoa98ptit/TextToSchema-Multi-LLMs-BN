Business Requirements Description:
Parking Management System:
1.When a vehicle enters the parking lot, the system queries available parking spaces and assigns the vehicle to an empty spot. If there are no available spots, the system returns null.
2.When a vehicle leaves the parking lot, the system marks the parking spot used by the vehicle as empty.
Supplementary Information:
The parking space table records the status of each parking space (whether it is occupied or not).
The entry table records the status of each entry point (whether a vehicle has entered).
The exit table records the status of each exit point (whether a vehicle has left).
