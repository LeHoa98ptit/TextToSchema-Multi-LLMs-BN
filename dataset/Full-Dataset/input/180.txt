Business Requirements Description for Pizza Shop Management System:
A pizza shop offers a variety of pizzas, each of which has a Pizza ID, Pizza Name, Price, and Stock Quantity. The shop has multiple customers, and their Customer ID, Customer Name, Customer Phone, and Customer Address are recorded. The shop processes multiple orders, each of which has an Order ID, Customer ID, Order Date, Expected Delivery Date, and Order Status. Each order records the pizzas ordered and their quantities.
