To facilitate management, a fishing company has decided to introduce a database for tool management. This company manages various fishing tools, each with a unique tool ID, name, and model. The company needs to record each tool's usage, including usage time, user, and location. The system also needs to record maintenance details for the tools, including maintenance time, maintenance content, and the person performing maintenance. Additionally, the system should be able to assess the performance of each tool, including usage frequency and maintenance counts.
