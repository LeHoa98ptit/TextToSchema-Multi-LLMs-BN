The business requirement description for the blog management system: A company plans to develop a blog or content management system to manage articles, authors, and category information. The system shall support the creation, editing, and deletion of articles, recording the Article ID, Name, Author ID, and Category ID. It shall associate author and category information through the Author ID and Category ID fields respectively, and support querying articles by author or category. An article may belong to multiple categories. The system shall record the category name and assign it a Category ID. The system shall also record basic information about authors, including the Author ID, Name, and Email.
