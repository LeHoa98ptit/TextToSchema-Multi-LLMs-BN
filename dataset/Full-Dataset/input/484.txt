The Agricultural Supply Chain Management System needs to manage multiple suppliers and customers.
Each supplier has a Supplier ID, name, address, contact information, and can supply multiple products.
Each customer has a Customer ID, name, address, contact information, and can purchase multiple products.
Each product has a Product ID, product name, and unit price.
Record the products that a supplier can supply.
Record each purchase, including the purchase date, Supplier ID, Customer ID, Product ID, and quantity.
