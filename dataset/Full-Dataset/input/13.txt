The Agricultural Meteorological Data Management System needs to record meteorological data from multiple weather stations. Each weather station can observe weather changes.
- Each weather station has a Weather Station ID, name, location, person in charge, and contact information.
- Each record includes Weather Station ID, recording date, temperature, humidity, precipitation, wind speed, and wind direction.
- Each farm can subscribe to data from multiple weather stations, recording the subscription date and unsubscription date.
