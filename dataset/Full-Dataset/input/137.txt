In an e-commerce system,
users can browse products and add them to the shopping cart, select the product quantity and apply discounts. When generating an order, the shipping address and payment method need to be specified.
The system includes product information management. Each product has a unique Product ID, Product Name, Original Price, and Discounted Price.       
Order information includes Order Number, User ID, Shipping Address, Amount Paid, Order Time, Payment Time, and Order Status.
The shopping cart records User ID, Product ID, Product Quantity, Discount Amount, and Total Amount.
Please design the database for this e-commerce system based on the above description.
