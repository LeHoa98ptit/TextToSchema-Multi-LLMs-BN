System Description
Describes a school management system

Main functions of the system
1: Student management: including basic information of students (such as student ID, name, gender, age) and accommodation information (such as dormitory number, building number, number of people, administrator)
2: Class management: including basic information of classes (such as class number) and classroom information
3: Sports team management: including basic information of sports teams (such as team name, activity time, coach, activity venue)
4: School management: including basic information of schools (such as school name) and information of sports teams
