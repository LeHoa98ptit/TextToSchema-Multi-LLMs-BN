Retail Management System

1. Supplier Management
Record the basic information of suppliers, including supplier ID name, address, and phone number.
You can query and manage supplier information.

2. Customer Management
Record the basic information of customers, including customer ID, first name, last name, phone number, address, email, etc.
You can query and manage customer information.

3. Product Management
Record the detailed information of products, including name, description, purchase price, selling price, inventory quantity, and minimum inventory quantity.
By associating supplier information with supplier ID, you can track the supplier of the product.
You can query and manage product information, including inventory management.

4. Invoice Management
Record the detailed information of each invoice, including invoice ID, date, tax amount, total value, and customer information.
By associating customer information with customer ID number, you can track the customer of the invoice.
You can generate and manage invoices.

5. Invoice Product Association Management
Record the products and quantities contained in each invoice.
Associating invoice and product information with invoice ID and product ID.
You can query the products and quantities contained in each invoice, as well as the detailed information of the products.
