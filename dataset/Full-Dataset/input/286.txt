The theater management system needs to manage information about performances, actors, and venues, and record the participation of actors in each performance. Specifically:
For each performance, the system shall record the name, start time, end time, and venue.
The theater has multiple venues, each with a name, address, and seating capacity.
The theater also has multiple actors, each with a name, email address, and phone number.
Each performance will invite some actors to participate.
