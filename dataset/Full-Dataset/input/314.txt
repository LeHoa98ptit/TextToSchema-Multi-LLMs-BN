Business Requirements Description:
This database is for an HR (Human Resources) Management System used within an organization. The system can:
1.Manage Department Information: Add, delete, and modify department details, including department ID, name, and description.
2.Manage Role Information: Add, delete, and modify roles within departments, including role name and salary. Each department can have multiple roles, and each role can exist in multiple departments.
3.Manage Employee Information: Add, delete, and modify employee details. The system can query an employee list with detailed information, including name, role, actual salary, and department.
4.Salary Management: Manage and modify employee salary information, including salary amount and leave status.
