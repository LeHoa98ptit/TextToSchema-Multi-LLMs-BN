The following is a description of a Residential Housekeeping Service Company System: The company provides housekeeping services such as cleaning, cooking, and care. The company has multiple service teams, with each team having multiple housekeepers. Housekeeper information includes staff ID, name, contact number, service type, years of experience, and professional skills. Each service task has a task ID, customer information (customer name, customer phone), service date, service address, service type, and service duration. After service completion, the system needs to record service fee, service effectiveness, and customer evaluation. Housekeepers' performance evaluation is based on the number of completed tasks and customer satisfaction.
