This is a sales management system for recording salespeople's performance and customer information. For sales record management, the system records detailed information of each sale, including sales amount, sales date, transaction quantity, and number of visits, and links sales records to specific salespeople. For salesperson management, the system stores basic information about salespeople, including unique identifier and name. For customer management, the system stores basic information about customers, including unique identifier and name. Through these functions, the system can help enterprises better manage their sales teams and customer relationships, improving sales efficiency and customer satisfaction.
