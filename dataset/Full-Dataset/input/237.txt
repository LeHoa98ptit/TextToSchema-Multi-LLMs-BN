This database is a house rental management system that can achieve the following main functions:

1: Management of district and street information: You can add, delete, and modify district and street information to ensure that the geographical classification of house rental information is accurate.

2: House type management: Define different house types, such as single room, whole rental, etc., so that users can choose the right house according to their needs.

3: House information management: Record the detailed information of each house, including the street, type, description and price, etc., so that users can find and understand the details of the house.

4: User management: Manage the user's registration information, including username, password and email, to ensure the information security and privacy of each user.

5: Rental management: Record the user's rental behavior, including the rented house, price, description and rental date, to facilitate tracking of rental status and follow-up services.

6: Query function: Support users to query house information according to conditions such as district, street, house type, etc., and view rental records.

7: Rental record statistics: You can count the rental status of each area, street or house type to provide decision support for managers.
