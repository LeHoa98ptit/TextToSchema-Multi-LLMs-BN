The supermarket management system is designed as follows:
The company owns multiple supermarkets, each with manager, address, and telephone information.
Each supermarket has multiple departments, with name, manager, and contact telephone for each department.
Each department employs multiple employees, with employee number, name, contact information, position, and salary for each employee.
The supermarket sells multiple products, with product number, name, model, unit price, and inventory quantity for each product.
The system needs to record the process of allocating products to salespersons, including allocation time, allocation quantity, and salesperson information such as salesperson number, name, contact information, etc.
Through this data, the system can manage the supermarket's operations, including employee management, product inventory, and allocation, to optimize business processes and improve efficiency.
