The agricultural machinery and equipment management system needs to manage the machinery and equipment of multiple farms. Each equipment has equipment ID, name, model, purchase date, usage status (such as "normal", "maintenance", "disabled", etc.), maintenance record and operator. Each farm has farm ID, name, address and contact information. Each maintenance record includes equipment ID, maintenance date, maintenance type (such as "routine inspection", "fault repair", etc.), operator and maintenance result (such as "completed", "uncompleted", etc.). It is necessary to record the basic information of the operator (operator ID, name, phone number) to facilitate the tracing of operation records.
