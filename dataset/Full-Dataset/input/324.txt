The database is a geolocation system used to determine the user's geographic location information based on the IP address. Such systems are often used in websites and applications to provide services or content based on geographic location.

Main functions

1. Mapping of IP address to geographic location:
Determine the user's geographic location through the IP address (network), including information such as continent and country.
Support querying whether the IP address belongs to an anonymous proxy or satellite provider.

2. Geographic location information query:
Provide detailed geographic location information, such as country name, ISO code, whether it is in the European Union, etc.
Support quick search of geographic location information by country ISO code.

3. Data management and maintenance:
Support importing and updating geographic location data and IP network data.
Ensure data consistency and integrity through Foreign key relationships.
