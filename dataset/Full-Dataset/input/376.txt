Business Requirements Description:
This system is designed to manage customer information, repair tasks, orders, parts, suppliers, and technicians for an auto repair service. The core functionalities include:
The system stores basic customer information, including a unique customer ID, name, and phone number.
The system maintains basic information for each technician, including a unique technician ID, name, and contact address.
The system stores information about different car models, each with a unique model ID and name.
Each repair task is recorded with a unique task ID, related car model ID, task status (e.g., Pending, In Progress, Completed), customer ID, technician ID, task creation date, and completion date.
Each repair task corresponds to an order, and the system records the order ID, related task ID, order creation date, and delivery status.
The system maintains basic information about suppliers, including a unique supplier ID and name.
The system tracks parts by their unique part ID, serial number, description, price, supplier ID, and inventory quantity.
The system manages the relationship between parts and orders, tracking the quantity of parts required for each order and the parts used in each repair task.
