Business Requirement Description:
The following describes a Sales Point Management System. The system’s main features might include:
1.Sales Plan Management: The company creates reasonable sales plans for sales points. The main Attributes include sales plan name, sales plan description, start date, and end date.
2.Sales Point Management: Each sales point includes a unique sales point ID, name, and address. Each sales point is associated with a specific venue, company, and city.
3.Device Management: Each device includes a device ID, device name, and device model. Each device is assigned to a corresponding sales point for use.
4.City Management: Each sales point is associated with a city, which includes a city ID, city name, and province.
5.Company Management: Each sales point is associated with a company, which includes a company ID, company name, and company address.
6.Venue Management: Each sales point is associated with a venue, which includes a venue ID, venue name, and venue description.
