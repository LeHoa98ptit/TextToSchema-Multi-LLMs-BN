This is a customer management system. For customer management, the system maintains basic customer information (customer ID, customer name, contact person, etc.), records detailed customer information (address, phone, email, etc.), and manages customer classification information (customer type, credit rating). For product management, the system maintains basic product information (product ID, product name, specifications, etc.), records detailed product information (unit price, inventory quantity, production date, etc.), and manages product classification information (product category, brand, supplier, etc.). For purchase management, the system records order information (order time, order status, etc.), maintains order details (product quantity, unit price, total amount, etc.), and manages payment information (payment method, payment status, payment time, etc.).
