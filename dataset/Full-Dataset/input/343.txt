Personnel Management: The system records basic information for personnel, including name and address (street and city). Each employee is identified by a unique ID. Each personnel may have multiple phone numbers; the system records this information and supports multiple records.

Vehicle Management: The system records detailed information for vehicles, including Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), manufacturer, model, year, and driver information. Each vehicle is identified by a unique VIN, and the system supports assigning personnel as drivers for vehicles. Drivers are linked to the Personnel table by a Foreign key to the ID and are updated through cascading updates.

Insurance Management: The system records insurance information for each vehicle, detailing each vehicle's purchased insurance and the specifics of the insurance (Insurance Type ID, Insurance Company Name, Insurance Cost, Insurance Content), along with the specific time of purchase and the validity period of the insurance for the vehicle.

Phone Management: The system allows recording multiple phone numbers for each personnel. Each phone number is associated with personnel via ID and uniquely identified by a composite Primary key of ID and Number. Each phone number's information includes: ID (Foreign key) and Number (phone number).
