This system is an automobile sales management system, which is mainly used to manage the information of companies, categories and dealers. The system allows users to add, delete and update company information, and each company has a unique ID and name. At the same time, category information can also be managed by category ID and company ID to ensure that each category belongs to a company, and when a company is deleted, all categories under the company will also be deleted. The dealer information includes dealer ID, name and company ID, which supports dealers to belong to a company or not to any company (that is, the company ID can be empty). When a company is deleted, the system will set the company ID of all dealers under the company to NULL. Companies and categories, as well as companies and dealers are associated through Foreign key to ensure data integrity and consistency.
