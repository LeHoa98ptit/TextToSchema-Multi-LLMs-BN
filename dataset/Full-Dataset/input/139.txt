Hospital Information System

Main functions of the system:
1. Department management: manage hospital department information, including department name, floor, etc.
2. Doctor management: manage doctor information, including doctor number, name, gender, title, etc.
3. Patient management: manage patient information, including patient name, age, gender, etc.
4. Diagnosis management: record the doctor's diagnosis information on the patient
5. Prescription management: record the prescription information issued by the doctor for the patient
6. Drug management: manage drug information, including drug name, price, manufacturer, etc.
7. Drug list management: record the drugs and their quantities included in the prescription
