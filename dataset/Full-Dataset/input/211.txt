Consulting Service System Business Requirement Description:
The Technical Consulting Department provides multiple technical consulting service projects, each of which involves multiple consultants. Consulting service projects have a Project ID, Name, Start Date, End Date, and Client Name. Consultants have a Consultant ID, Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Phone Number, and Professional Field. Consulting service records need to record the Service ID, Project ID, Consultant ID, Service Date, and Service Content.
