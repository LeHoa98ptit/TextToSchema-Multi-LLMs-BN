Business Requirements Description for Airline Ticket Reservation System:
An airline operates multiple flights, each of which has a Flight ID, Airline, Departure City, Departure Time, Destination, Arrival Time, and Ticket Price. The company records discount information, including Flight ID, Discount Start Date, Discount End Date, and Discount Rate. The company manages multiple passenger records, each of which includes Passenger ID, Passenger Name, Passenger Gender, Passenger Date of Birth, Passenger Phone, and VIP Discount. Each ticket purchase is recorded with a Ticket Order Number, Passenger ID, Flight ID, Travel Date, and Purchase Amount. The purchase amount may be influenced by flight discounts and VIP discounts.
