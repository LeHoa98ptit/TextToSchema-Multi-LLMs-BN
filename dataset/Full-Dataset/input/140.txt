This is a Product Management System, mainly used for managing products, staff, suppliers, and customer information. For product information management, the system records basic product information, including product ID, product name, price, and inventory quantity. For staff management, the system maintains employee basic information, including employee ID and name, while also recording employee position, employment status, and department information. For supplier management, the system manages supplier basic information, including supplier ID and name, and records supplier addresses and types of products supplied. For customer management, the system maintains customer basic information, including customer ID and name.
