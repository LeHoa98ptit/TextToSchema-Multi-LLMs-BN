the database is an aircraft information management system, primarily used for managing airlines' aircraft, flights, crew members, maintenance records, and passenger information. the main functions of the system include recording basic aircraft information, flight scheduling, crew allocation, maintenance records; tracking passengers' flight reservations and ticket purchases; providing inquiries into aircraft maintenance history and flight information.

the system stores basic aircraft information.
fields: aircraft id, aircraft model, manufacture date, maximum passenger capacity, aircraft status, affiliated airline
the system records each aircraft's basic data, such as aircraft model, manufacture date, maximum passenger capacity, current status (e.g., under maintenance, awaiting flight, in-flight), and associates it with the affiliated airline.

the system stores information of the crew members (including captain, co-pilot, etc.).
fields: crew member id, name, gender, position, phone
the system records crew members' personal information, including name, position, and contact details, facilitating the management of crew members for each flight.

the system stores basic passenger information.
fields: passenger id, name, gender, identification number, phone
the system records detailed passenger information, such as name, gender, identification number, and contact details, supporting passenger identification management and flight arrangements.

the system records detailed flight information, including departure and landing times, flight number, etc.
fields: flight id, aircraft id, responsible captain id, departure airport, landing airport, departure time, landing time
this data is used to manage basic flight information, including flight number, departure and landing airports, aircraft id, and flight times, associating the aircraft id with the aircraft table and responsible captain id with the crew member table.

the system records the maintenance history of aircraft.
fields: maintenance personnel id, aircraft id, maintenance date, maintenance description, maintenance type
this data associates maintenance personnel (crew members) and aircraft, recording maintenance date, maintenance details, etc., to facilitate tracing the maintenance history of aircraft.

the system records passenger ticket purchase information.
fields: passenger id, flight id, booking time, seat number, ticket price
the system records passenger booking information, including the purchased flight, seat number, ticket price, and booking time, associating passengers with flights.
