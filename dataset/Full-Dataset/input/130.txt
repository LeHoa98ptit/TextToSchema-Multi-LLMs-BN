Database design for a company is as follows:
The company has multiple corporate and individual clients.
Corporate client information includes corporate tax ID, corporate name, postal code, corporate size, and corporate type.
Individual client information includes person's name, date of birth, and personal tax ID.
Each client can be a corporate client or an individual client, and client information is stored in corresponding tables.
The company conducts several projects, project information includes project value, start date, end date, project status, service type, and whether it is a digital project.
Projects contain multiple members, member information includes name, position, domain, date of hire, and date of departure, and members can participate in multiple projects simultaneously.
