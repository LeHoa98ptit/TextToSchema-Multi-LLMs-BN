A national agency requires management of the document circulation process, including approval, archiving, and querying of documents.
Documents have a document ID, name, category, creation time, and status;
Approval records include document ID, approver, approval time, and approval comments;
Archiving records include document ID, archivist, archiving time, and archiving location;
Query records include document ID, query personnel, query time, and query results;
The approval and querying operations of documents can occur multiple times, whereas archiving occurs only once.
Document management personnel are responsible for approving, archiving, and querying documents; they have employee ID, name, gender, position, and contact information.
