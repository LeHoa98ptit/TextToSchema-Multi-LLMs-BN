The Credit Card Management System for a credit card company requires managing various types of credit cards and associated transactions:
The company issues multiple types of credit cards, each with a unique Card Type Code, Card Type Name, and Annual Fee.
The company has several customers, each identified by a unique Customer ID, Name, ID Number, and Contact Number. Each customer can have multiple credit cards.
Each credit card has a unique Card Number, Card Type Code, Account Opening Date, and Credit Limit. A customer may have several credit cards.
Every transaction made using a credit card must record the Amount, Transaction Time, Transaction Location, and Transaction Type.
The system also tracks repayments made by customers, including the Repayment Amount and Repayment Time.
