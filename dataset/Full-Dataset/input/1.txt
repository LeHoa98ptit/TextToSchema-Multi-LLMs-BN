The airline company operates as follows:
The company owns multiple airplanes, each with an Airplane Number, Model, and Passenger Capacity.
Each airplane can perform multiple flights. Flight information includes Flight Number, Departure Time, Arrival Time, Origin, Destination, Airplane Number, Captain ID, First Officer ID, Start Time, and End Time.
Each flight is operated by a captain and a first officer, each having their own ID, Name, and Phone Number.
