System Requirements Function Description

1. Front-end Function
Film and TV Information Browsing: Users can browse the latest movie and TV series information
Film and TV Reviews: Users can comment on movies and TV series
Film and TV Sharing: Users can share their favorite movies and TV series
Film and TV Rankings: Users can view the most popular movie and TV series rankings
Film and TV Search: Users can search for movies and TV series of interest by keywords
Crew Information Query: Users can query the crew information of movies and TV series, such as directors, actors, etc.
User Login: Users can register and log in to the website to enjoy more personalized services
Crew Login: Crew members can register and log in to the website to publish and manage relevant information
User Registration: Users can register to become website members
Crew Registration: Crew members can register to become website members

2. Backstage functions
General user management: Administrators can manage the account information of registered users, such as adding, deleting, and modifying user information
Crew information management: Administrators can manage the account information of registered crews, such as adding, deleting, and modifying crew information
Administrator information management: Administrators can manage their own account information, such as changing passwords, viewing logs, etc.
News information management: Administrators can publish, edit, and delete news information
News column management: Administrators can add, delete, and modify news columns
Film and television information publishing management: Administrators can publish, edit, and delete information about movies and TV series
Comment information management: Administrators can view, review, and delete user comment information
Film and television ranking management: Administrators can adjust the ranking rules for movies and TV series
System maintenance: Administrators can perform basic system maintenance, such as backing up the database, updating the system, etc.
