"The system is designed for a corporate group to manage the business operations of its multiple large chain stores. Key features include:
1. Record store information, including Store ID, Store Name, Address, and Contact Phone Number.
2. Record department information for each store, including Department ID, Department Name, Location Distribution, and Contact Phone Number.
3. Record employee information for each department, including Employee ID, Name, Position, Phone Number, and Salary.
4. Record manager information for each department."
