The diagram depicts a vehicle management system. The main functions of the system may include:

1. User management: including adding, modifying, deleting and querying user information.
2. Vehicle information management: including adding, modifying, deleting and querying vehicle information.
3. Vehicle maintenance management: including adding, modifying, deleting and querying maintenance records.
4. Vehicle departure management: including adding, modifying, deleting and querying departure records.
5. Vehicle maintenance application management: including submitting, reviewing and querying maintenance applications.
6. Vehicle repair shop management: including adding, modifying, deleting and querying repair shop information.
7. Announcement information management: including publishing, modifying, deleting and querying announcements.
8. Token information management: including token generation, verification and expiration processing.

The corresponding database model is as follows:

1. User information table: including fields such as user ID, user name, password, role, etc.

2. Vehicle information table: including fields such as vehicle ID, vehicle name, vehicle photo, vehicle status, vehicle category, etc.
3. Vehicle maintenance table: including fields such as maintenance ID, vehicle ID, maintenance time, maintenance details, whether to review, review reply, etc.
4. Vehicle departure table: including fields such as departure ID, vehicle ID, departure time, departure notes, etc.
5. Vehicle maintenance application form: including fields such as application ID, vehicle ID, application time, application reason, whether to review, review reply, etc.
6. Repair shop information table: including fields such as repair shop ID, shop name, contact person, contact number, etc.
7. Announcement information table: including fields such as announcement ID, title, content, creation time, etc.
8. Token information table: including fields such as tokenID, token value, creation time, expiration time, etc.
