The design of a technology conference management system is as follows:
The conference has participants, whose information includes Participant ID, Name, Company, and Email.
The conference offers various types of tickets, with ticket information including Ticket ID, Name, Description, Price, and whether it includes workshops, with each participant limited to purchasing one ticket.
Participants can use discount codes to purchase tickets, with discount code information including Code, Name, Type, and Amount.
After purchasing tickets, participant ticket records are generated, containing Participant ID, Ticket ID, Discount Code ID, and Net Price.
The conference schedules multiple meetings, with meeting information including Name, Description, Date, Start Time, End Time, and Room.
Furthermore, the conference also offers workshops, with workshop information including Name, Description, Requirements, Location, Date, Start Time, End Time, and Capacity.
Each meeting or workshop can have multiple speakers, with speaker information including Name, Company, and Description.
