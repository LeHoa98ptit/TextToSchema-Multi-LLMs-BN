This database is intended to record information related to political parties, candidates, voters, and voting activities. The system will support the following operations:
Political Parties: Record the basic information about political parties, including name and description. The system should support the ability to add, delete, and modify political party information.
Candidates: Record basic information about candidates, including their name, the political party they belong to, and whether they have industry connections. The system should allow adding, deleting, and modifying candidate information.
Voters: Record basic information about voters, including their name, email, and the creation time of their registration. The system should support adding, deleting, and modifying voter information.
Voting Records: Record each voting activity, including the voter ID, candidate ID, and the time of voting. The system ensures that each voter can only vote once.
