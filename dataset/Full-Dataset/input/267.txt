Description of the business requirements of the hotel reservation system:

The hotel has multiple rooms, each with a room number, type (such as standard room, deluxe room), and price. Customers can reserve a room, and when making a reservation, they need to record the customer's name, phone number, reservation date, check-in date, and check-out date. The hotel needs to record the room information, customer information, and reservation status (such as confirmed, cancelled) for each reservation.
