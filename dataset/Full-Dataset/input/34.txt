  A civil engineering company owns multiple pieces of equipment. Each piece of equipment has an equipment ID, equipment name, model, purchase date, purchase price, and status. The company has multiple projects. Each project has a project ID, project name, project description, and creation time. Each project can use multiple pieces of equipment. Equipment usage records need to include project ID, equipment ID, usage date, usage time, and user. The equipment management information system needs to be able to record equipment and equipment usage.
