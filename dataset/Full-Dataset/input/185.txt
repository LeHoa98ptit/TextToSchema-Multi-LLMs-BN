The People's Political Consultative Conference requires a system to manage proposals from its members. Each proposal includes a Proposal ID, Proposal Content, Proposal Time, Proposer(s), and Status. A proposer can consist of multiple members, each with a Member ID, Name, Gender, Date of Birth, Party Affiliation, and Position. The system also needs to record the handling of each proposal, which includes Handling Time, Handler, and Handling Opinion. The handler is a member different from the proposer.
